JetBrains presentation template

Your presentations are an important part of the JetBrains brand. Since they are often used to share business thoughts, ideas, and plans, it is critical that they conform to and promote a unified and professional image for the company – both internally and externally. Our presentation template is designed to help you make sure they do just that.

For detailed information on how to use this template efficiently, please refer to the guidelines.

Please take note that presentations are intended to help the audience focus on the speaker’s words rather than distract them with detailed reading material. Each slide should use only a handful of words to reiterate the speaker's points, using imagery and text that reinforce the speaker’s point. Avoid inserting so much text that you have to decrease the font size.

If the presentation is intended to be used in conjunction with a pre-recorded video of you speaking, please use these templates.

Theme colors

The JetBrains Brand Colors have been added to the presentation for ease of use. Keep the colors simple and consistent with good contrast.

Only use colors from the corporate color palette.

Slide with Math

Lorenz system:
\[ \begin{align*} \frac{dx}{dt} &= \sigma(y - x) \\ \frac{dy}{dt} &= x(\rho - z) - y \\ \frac{dz}{dt} &= xy - \beta z \end{align*} \]
Maxwell's equations:
\[ \begin{align*} \nabla \cdot \mathbf{E} & = \frac {\rho} {\varepsilon_0} \quad \\ \nabla \cdot \mathbf{B} & = 0 \quad \\ \nabla \times \mathbf{E} & = -\frac{\partial \mathbf{B}}{\partial t} \quad \\ \nabla \times \mathbf{B} & = \mu_0\mathbf{J} + \mu_0\varepsilon_0\frac{\partial \mathbf{E}}{\partial t} \quad \end{align*} \]
